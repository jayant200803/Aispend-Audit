## Cursor

**Source:** https://cursor.com/pricing  
**Verified:** May 2026

| Plan | Price/seat/mo | Notes |
|------|--------------|-------|
| Hobby | $0 | Limited completions, limited agent requests |
| Pro | $20 | $20 in frontier model credits, unlimited Tab, cloud agents |
| Pro+ | $60 | 3x usage on all models vs Pro |
| Ultra | $200 | 20x usage on all models vs Pro |
| Teams | $40/user | Shared admin, centralized billing, SAML/OIDC SSO |
| Enterprise | Custom | Invoice billing, pooled usage, advanced security |

**Key notes:** Cursor moved from request-based to usage-based credits in June 2025. The Pro plan includes $20 in API credits for frontier model selection; Auto mode is unlimited and does not draw from credits. Teams pricing doubled from $20 to $40/user during the June 2025 pricing overhaul.

## Notes on Credex Savings Estimate

StackAudit estimates an additional 15% savings via Credex discounted credits. This figure is conservative — Credex sources credits from companies that overforecast their AI usage, passing savings to customers. Actual savings vary by tool and volume; the 15% figure is used as a consistent floor estimate.
