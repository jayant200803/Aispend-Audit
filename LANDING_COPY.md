# Landing Copy

Copy variations for StackAudit's landing page, email subject lines, and social posts.

---

## Headlines (tested in user interviews)

### Version A (current, live)
**H1:** Stop overpaying for AI tools  
**H2:** Find out if you're overspending on AI tools. Get a free, instant audit of your Cursor, Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT & API spend — with real savings recommendations.

**Why it works:** Direct problem statement. "Stop" is an action word. "Real savings recommendations" sets an expectation (not vague "insights").

---

### Version B (curiosity-driven)
**H1:** Is your team wasting money on AI tools?  
**H2:** Most startups overpay by 20–40% because they're on the wrong plans. Takes 60 seconds to find out.

**Why it works:** Question headline creates curiosity. "Most startups" creates social proof. "60 seconds" sets a low time commitment expectation.

---

### Version C (specific)
**H1:** Cursor + Copilot + Claude. What's it actually costing you?  
**H2:** Enter your AI tools and we'll show you exactly where the money is going — and how to cut it.

**Why it works:** Specific tool names signal relevance immediately. Engineers recognize these products and self-select in.

---

### Version D (outcome-focused)
**H1:** Save $800/month on AI tools  
**H2:** Free audit in 60 seconds. No login required. Based on actual pricing, not ballpark guesses.

**Why it works:** Specific dollar amount creates a concrete expectation. "No login required" is a trust signal.

**Recommendation for A/B test:** A vs. D. Version A is honest and empathetic; Version D leads with a specific outcome. Both are true, but D is more conversion-optimized.

- ❌ "Unlock savings" (vague corporate speak)
- ❌ "Maximize your ROI" (cliché)
- ❌ "Comprehensive platform" (we're a free audit tool, not a platform)
- ❌ "Industry-leading" (unsupported claim)
- ❌ "End-to-end solution" (meaningless in this context)

## FAQ — 5 Real Q&As

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**
A: It's genuinely free with no catch. StackAudit is built by Credex, a company that sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. We offer the audit free because it helps us find companies that are overpaying for AI tools — the ones most likely to benefit from Credex. You get a real audit; we get qualified leads. If you have significant savings, we'll surface Credex as an option. If you don't, we'll just tell you you're spending well.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: All pricing is verified weekly against official vendor pricing pages (Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Windsurf). Every number in the audit engine traces to a cited URL with a verification date in PRICING_DATA.md. We don't estimate — if a price isn't available on the official page, we don't include it. That said, enterprise pricing is negotiated, so your actual contract may differ from list price — use the manual spend override field for custom pricing.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No. You run the audit, see results, and optionally submit your email to get the report sent to you. No account, no login, no password. Your audit is stored under a unique URL you can bookmark or share.

**Q: What happens to my data?**
A: Your tool selections, spend figures, and savings results are stored in our database to generate your shareable report URL. If you submit your email, it's stored securely and used only to send your audit report and relevant Credex updates — never sold to third parties. The public share URL strips all identifying information (email, company name) and shows only tool categories and savings figures.

**Q: What if I negotiate custom pricing with a vendor?**
A: Use the manual spend override field. Every tool entry has a "Monthly ($)" input that you can edit directly — it defaults to plan price × seats, but you can type in your actual spend. The audit engine uses whatever number you enter, so custom contracts are fully supported.
